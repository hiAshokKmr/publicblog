Image Upload

Drag and Drop Image:

Go to your Computer, Right click the Image You want to add and Drag it into the editor and Drop it

Copy/Paste Image:

Go to your computer, Right click the Image You want to add then copy it with Ctrl + c and and paste it with Ctrl + v inside Editor

Click/Upload Image:

Click the Image icon in the Editor, Goto Upload in Image Properties, click Browse the image, and Send it to Server, Then click ok
